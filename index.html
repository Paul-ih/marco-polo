In the US, Marco Polo is a fun swimming pool game.
The main player closes his eyes and shouts “Marco!”.  
The other players have to reply “Polo!”,
while he tries to locate them by ear and tag them.

Move the cursor with the arrow keys. Try to
locate the target by using one of
the keys for “MARCO”. The redder the screen,
the closer you are. Be fast, the target is also moving!
